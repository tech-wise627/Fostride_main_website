The Problem

The waste stream is broken.

India generates 62 million tonnes of municipal solid waste annually. Less than 20% is processed scientifically. The rest ends up in landfills — not because we don't care, but because we lack the intelligence to sort at scale.

🏛
NIDHI Grant Recipient
Dept. of Science & Technology, Govt. of India
01 /

Manual Sorting Fails at Scale

Human sorters average <60% accuracy. It's inconsistent, hazardous, and expensive — especially in high-throughput environments.

02 /

Zero Data, Zero Accountability

No organisation knows what's in their bins. Without data, ESG reporting is guesswork. Recyclers can't route efficiently.

03 /

Recyclers Work Blind

Contaminated batches arrive without manifest. Up to 40% of "recyclables" end in landfill due to poor sorting upstream.

The Solution

Waste intelligence,
built to compound.

W.I.S.E. is the AI layer that sits at the bin. Every disposal event is a data point. Every data point makes the model smarter. The more waste flows through it, the more accurate and valuable it becomes.

🤖

Computer Vision at the Bin

On-device AI classifies every item — plastics, paper, organic, e-waste — in under 500ms. No cloud round-trip.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

Every sort becomes a structured data point — category, weight, timestamp, location. Live dashboard, automatic ESG reports.

🔄

Closed-Loop Routing

Sorted streams are routed directly to verified recyclers and upcyclers. Contamination drops. Value recovered increases.

Process

Four steps.
Zero effort.

01

Drop the Waste

User tosses waste into the R3Bin. Camera activates. The process begins.

02

AI Identifies & Sorts

W.I.S.E. classifies the item in <500ms. Servo motors route it to the correct compartment automatically.

03

Data Captured & Tracked

Category, weight, fill-level, timestamp — all logged. Organisations get a live dashboard and audit trail.

04

Routed to Recyclers

Sorted batches are matched to verified recyclers and upcyclers. The loop closes. Nothing goes to landfill unnecessarily.
